James Mulholland's latest project:

21st-Century Renaissance Man created with the help of Artificial Intelligence; Art & Information Design with AI assistance. With art made with the help of AI, we are changing the way we see the world. I’m using artificial intelligence for efficiency, speed, and muse. Using artificial intelligence provides an experimental chance to challenge one’s perceptions, perspectives, and assumptions. Because the context of my art has an impact on the art’s relationship with the viewer the viewer is placed in a position where they must confront their assumptions and biases, providing an opportunity for growth. My use of artificial intelligence is an invitation to question and reflect on the assumptions we each hold. The Story Behind

I create art that will inspire people to see the world in a new light. Come see what I can do.
