James Mulholland's latest project:

The 21st-Century Renaissance Man is created with the help of Artificial Intelligence (AI) for efficiency, speed, and muse; Art & Information Design with AI assistance. With art made with the help of AI, we are changing the way we see the world. Using AI provides an experimental chance to challenge one’s perceptions, perspectives, and assumptions. Because the context of my art has an impact on the art’s relationship with the viewer, the viewer is placed in a position where they must confront their assumptions and biases, providing an opportunity for growth. My use of artificial intelligence is an invitation to question and reflect on the assumptions we each hold. The Story Behind the Project

I create art that will inspire people to see the world in a new light. Come see what I can do.
